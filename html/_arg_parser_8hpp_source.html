127  *\n - Options are arguments that begin with 2 dash '-' characters, and can capture additional arguments if the option name appears in the capture list.
128  *\n - Flags are arguments that begin with a single dash '-' character, are a single character in length, and can capture additional arguments. 129  *\n __Capture Rules__
130  *\n - Only options/flags specified in the capture list are allowed to capture additional arguments. Capture list entries should not include a delimiter prefix.
131  *\n - Options/Flags cannot be captured under any circumstance. ex: "--opt --opt captured" results in "--opt", & "--opt" + "captured".
132  *\n - If a flag in a chain should capture an argument (either with an '=' delimiter or by context), it must appear at the end of the chain.
