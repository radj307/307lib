◆ AlignType

enum token::parse::AlignType
strong

Controls the alignment of = signs in INIContainer's operator<< output.

Enumerator
NONE 

Inserts " = " between all keys and values.

SECTION 

Determines the longest key name in a section, and inserts " = " after that length, for all keys in the section.

ALL 

Determines the longest key name in the whole file, and inserts " = " after that length, for all keys.
