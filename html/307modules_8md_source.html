5 ## Usage
6 
7 First you need to tell CMake where to locate these modules, by appending the path to the 307modules directory to the `CMAKE_MODULE_PATH` list.
8 If you're using this through [307lib](https://github.com/radj307/307lib), you would use the following:
9 ```cmake
10 list(APPEND CMAKE_MODULE_PATH "${CMAKE_CURRENT_SOURCE_DIR}/307lib/307modules")
11 ```
12 _You may have to change the path depending on where 307modules is located._
13 
14 Now all of the modules are available to be included. To include a module, use:
15 ```cmake
16 include(<ModuleName>)
17 ```
18 where `<ModuleName>` is the filename of a module, excluding the `.cmake` extension.
