template<var::valid_char TChar = char, typename TCharTraits = std::char_traits<TChar>, typename TAlloc = std::allocator<TChar>>
struct color::setcolor_seq< TChar, TCharTraits, TAlloc >

Acts as a wrapper and controller for SGR & RGB color codes, as well as certain SGR format codes.
Uses the ostream operator<< to insert escape sequences into streams.
