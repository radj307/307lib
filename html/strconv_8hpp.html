Detailed Description

Contains char & string conversion functions with signatures similar to the standard atoi & stoi style functions, with additional type support.
This includes the tolower & toupper functions that have stricter validation & have overloads that accept whole strings rather than single characters.
Multiple 'generations' of these functions are available, but all have a common theme:

Author
radj307
  • Remove the possibility of exceptions being thrown during conversion operations.
  • Optimized for being passed as (pseudo)-lambdas to other functions, especially in an environment where exceptions are unwanted or unnecessary.
Attention
Make sure you carefully evaluate whether or not to use these functions outside of strlib, as in many cases the exception-avoidance measures taken by all of these functions may not be desirable.
This header is specifically designed to handle many potential errors behind-the-scenes without any indications, and may react unexpectedly when used as a drop-in replacement for standard library functions, despite their ability to do that.
